We're excited to announce the launch of **Assets Flow** - a new and advanced investment portfolio management app. Assets Flow is designed to help you track all your assets in one place, simply and efficiently.

## The Challenges of Investment Management

Managing an investment portfolio can be a complex task. Whether it's real estate, stocks, crypto, or other investments - tracking all assets, income, and expenses can be challenging. Many use spreadsheets, bank accounts, or different apps - creating clutter and disorganization.

## The Solution - Assets Flow

Assets Flow offers a comprehensive solution for all your investment management needs:

### Comprehensive Investment Tracking

Track all types of investments in one place:

- **Real Estate** - Manage properties, track rental income, expenses, and ROI
- **Stocks** - Monitor stock portfolios and bonds
- **Crypto** - Manage digital currencies and digital assets
- **Others** - Investment funds, debentures, and more

### Advanced Financials

View detailed financial data on every investment:

- Income and expense cash flows
- Annual and cumulative return calculations
- Profit and loss reports
- Advanced charts and analyses

### Project Management

Organize your investments by projects:

- Create projects for each asset or group of assets
- Track payments and expenses
- Manage documents and receipts
- Get alerts for upcoming payments

### Multi-Currency Support

Support for multiple currencies including:

- Israeli Shekel (₪)
- US Dollar ($)
- Euro (€)
- More currencies to be added in the future

## Security and Privacy

We understand that your financial data is sensitive and important. That's why Assets Flow uses:

- **Encryption** - All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Authentication** - Secure login with Google or email
- **Backup** - Automatic data backup
- **Privacy** - Your data remains yours, we never sell it to third parties

## What's Coming Next?

We're planning many expansions for Assets Flow, including:

- Support for additional currencies
- Integration with bank accounts and brokers
- More advanced reports
- Social features for sharing and consulting
