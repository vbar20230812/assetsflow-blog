## What is AssetFlow?

AssetFlow is an innovative investment management app designed to help you gain complete control over your investment portfolio.

### Key Features

- **Asset Tracking** - Manage all asset types in one place
- **Advanced Reports** - Insights and analytics for better decision-making
- **Monthly Cash Flow** - Track income and expenses from your assets
- **Return Calculation** - Automatic calculation of annual and monthly returns

### Why Choose AssetFlow?

Our app was developed with a simple approach: **make investment management accessible and transparent**.

We believe every investor, small or large, deserves professional tools to manage their portfolio.
